Featured Review: Kelty Granite 65 Backpack

Kelty Granite 65 Backpack

We recently put the Kelty Granite 65 Backpack to the test on a week-long backpacking trip through the Sierra Nevadas. This pack impressed us with its comfortable suspension system, durable construction, and abundance of compartments. Whether you're planning a multi-day trek or a weekend camping trip, the Kelty Granite 65 is a versatile option for backpackers of all experience levels.
