Recent Adventures and Events

Hiking the Inca Trail to Machu Picchu (Peru)

Embark on a legendary journey along the Inca Trail, a breathtaking path through the Andes Mountains leading to the iconic lost city of Machu Picchu. Our guide provides detailed trail maps, information on permits and logistics, along with insider tips on acclimatization, choosing the right gear, and capturing stunning photos of this awe-inspiring trek.

Kayaking the Whitsunday Islands (Australia)

Paddle through paradise on a kayaking adventure in the Whitsunday Islands, Australia. This guide offers everything you need to know, from planning your itinerary and selecting the best kayak tour to exploring hidden coves, snorkeling vibrant coral reefs, and spotting playful marine life.

Mountain Biking in Moab, Utah (USA)

Get your adrenaline pumping with a mountain biking adventure in Moab, Utah. Our comprehensive guide features a selection of the most exciting trails catering to all skill levels, from thrilling single-track descents to scenic loops through red rock canyons. Learn about bike rentals, safety tips, and local spots to refuel after conquering the trails.

Wildlife Safari in Kruger National Park (South Africa)

Immerse yourself in the wonders of African wildlife on a safari in Kruger National Park. This expert guide equips you with everything you need for an unforgettable experience: information on choosing the right safari type, identifying the incredible animals you might encounter, and tips for capturing breathtaking wildlife photography.
